## Announcement

FAIMI has again been selected to host a MICCAI 2026 workshop.

This year the workshop will be a joint **FAIMI** (Fairness), **BRIDGE** (Regulatory Evaluation), and **EPIMI** (Ethics & Philosophy) workshop. The joint workshop will integrate the unique expertise of FAIMI, BRIDGE, and EPIMI into a singular, interdisciplinary forum to collaboratively tackle these crucial issues.

The session is structured around three connected pillars necessary for trustworthy AI:

- **Fairness of Artificial Intelligence in Medical Image (FAIMI):** Defining, detecting, and mitigating systemic biases, including those based on sex, ethnicity, and geography, that introduce risks and inequity in biomedical image analysis.
- **Bridging Regulatory Science and Medical Imaging Evaluation (BRIDGE):** Developing novel approaches towards robust regulatory science for novel AI paradigms.
- **Ethical and Philosophical Issues in Medical Imaging (EPIMI):** Debating humanistic and philosophical approaches to elucidate new ethical and regulatory concerns and potential gaps in existing methods.

> Please note: we are closely following MICCAI's messaging regarding travel restrictions and related guidance.
